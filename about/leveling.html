Leveling system
General

In Thronefall you level up by playing the game and earning points, which are calculated using the following equation:

(base score + (gold amount x 10)) x 1,2 ^ (mutator amount)

By surpassing certain scores you level up and get new perks, weapons, upgrade paths or even permanent upgrades.
