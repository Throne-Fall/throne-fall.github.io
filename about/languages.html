Supported languages

Currently 10 Languages are supported by Thronefall. While the English and German translation was created by the developers, all the other languages were AI-translated. The devs have stated, that they will pay for proper translation when the game comes out fully / gets out of Early Access.

Available languages

  • English
  • French
  • German
  • Spanish - Spain
  • Japanese
  • Polish
  • Portuguese - Brazil
  • Russian
  • Simplified Chinese
  • Turkish
