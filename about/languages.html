Supported languages

Currently 14 Languages are supported by Thronefall. Earlier versions of the game made use of machine translations for languages other than German and English. However, as of update 1.75 all of the machine translations have been replaced by proper translations from the people over at Level Up Translation.

Available languages

  • English
  • French
  • German
  • Spanish - Spain
  • Japanese
  • Polish
  • Portuguese - Brazil
  • Russian
  • Simplified Chinese
  • Turkish
  • Italian
  • Czech
  • Korean
  • Ukrainian
