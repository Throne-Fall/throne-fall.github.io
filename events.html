Version 1.5
20th March 2024

Huge balancing update with many changes and reworks.

TOWERS REWORK:

  • Defense towers have brand new and reworked level 2 and level 3 upgrades.

ETERNAL TRIALS - SEASON 2:

  • Complete rebalancing of the eternal trials wave generator and a fresh start with an empty highscore table.
  • More difficult, new enemy preview, less random, more predictable, unwanted perks can be disabled.

QUALITY OF LIFE:

  • New unit control hotkeys, opening up huge micro potential and saving you tons of time during the day
  • New command hotkey that selects or places only one unit type at a time (allows for easy sorting of clumped up units)
  • Snappier keyboard controls and mouse movement added as a control option.
  • Next day gold preview in the pause menu
  • Tower range indicators

BALANCING:

Almost every perk and castle center upgrade has been touched in some shape or form. Some highlights:

  • Your berserks have a brand new jump attack and your flails have health steal.
  • Self-healing is a separate value now and no longer scales with your max health pool.
  • Quick slings have to stop firing to reload.
  • Mill upgrades are more powerful.
  • Way more than we can even list here...
Full patch notes on Steam
