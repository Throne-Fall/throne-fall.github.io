The game

Saddle the horses! See your kingdom come to life, fight gripping battles to defend it and still be done in time for lunch.
With Thronefall we tried to strip a classic strategy game from all unnecessary complexity, combining it with some healthy hack and slay. Build up your base during the day, defend it till your last breath at night.
