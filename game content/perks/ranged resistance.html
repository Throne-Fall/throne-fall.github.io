Ranged Resistance
General

The Ranged Resistance Perk is one of the currently 26 Perks in the game. It is unlocked upon reaching level 43 and reduces the amount of ranged damage your king, units and buildings take.


Description

You, your units and all of your buildings start the game with +35% resistance against ranged attacks.

Statistics

Any Damage from ranged attacks that the King, units and all buildings would take is multiplied by 0.741, resulting in a 26% damage reduction. This stacks multiplicatively with the Blacksmith Ranged Resistance upgrades.
