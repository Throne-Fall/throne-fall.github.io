Fortified Houses
General

The Fortified Houses Perk is one of the currently 26 Perks in the game. It is unlocked upon reaching level 35 and increases the durability of your houses. Level 2 houses also gain a ranged attack.


Description

Houses have +250% more health and level 2 houses shoot arrows at enemies dealing 8 damage to enemies nearby every 1.66 seconds.
