Magic Armor
General

Magic Armor is an upgrade path of the Castle Center. It can be chosen upon upgrading the Castle Center to level 2 and increases the health poins of the King significantly.

Magic Armor also reflects damage the King takes, dealing damage to enemies equal to the damage that the enemies dealt to the King.

Description

+Enemies that deal damage to you, take a part of that damage themselves. +150% HP for your character.
