Commander
General

Commander is an upgrade path of the Castle Center. It can be chosen upon upgrading the Castle Center to level 3, increases the damage of the King slightly and enables units to attack while being commanded by the player.

Commander also increases the movement speed of friendly units by +60% while being commanded.

Description
  • Units move 60% faster while you command them
  • Units can attack while you command them
  • 30% attack damage for your character
