Assassin's Training
General

Assassin's Training is an upgrade path of the Castle Center. It can be chosen upon upgrading the Castle Center to level 2 and increases the damage of the King significantly, but halves the King's attack speed.

Assassin's Training increases the Kings attack damage by +250% (350% total) at the cost of -50% attack speed.

Description

-Your auto-attack cooldown is +100% longer. +You deal +250% more damage.

The Assassin's Training Icon.
