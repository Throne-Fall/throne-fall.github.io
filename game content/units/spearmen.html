Spearmen
General

Spearmen are a basic melee unit in Thronefall. While first introduced in Norfels, they can be recruited everywhere in the kingdom of Thronefall.

Description

Spearmen are fast and strong units. They do extra damage against fast enemies and slow them down. Not a great pick against ranged units.
