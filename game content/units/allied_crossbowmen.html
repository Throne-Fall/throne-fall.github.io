Crossbowmen
General

Crossbowmen are a basic ranged unit in Thronefall. While first introduced in Norfels, they can be recruited everywhere in the kingdom of Thronefall.

Description

Crossbowmen have a low range, but high damage output. They are armored against ranged attacks. Bonus damage against ranged units. Less effective against flying units.
