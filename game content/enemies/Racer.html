Racer
General

Racers are a basic monster enemy in Thronefall. While not introduced in the tutorial, they can be encountered everywhere in the kingdom of Thronefall.

Description

Racers are fast rolling monsters, that are not messing around. They aim right for your castle center and are somewhat armored against attacks from the player character.
