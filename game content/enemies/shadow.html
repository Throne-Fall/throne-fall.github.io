Shadow In The Water
General

The Shadow In The Water is a boss enemy in Thronefall. They are encountered in the colder regions of the kingdom of Thronefall.

Unlike all the other enemies in Thronefall, the Shadow In The Water spawns units instead of dealing damage directly. It takes damage every time it does so.
