Blacksmiths
General

Blacksmiths are a protective building in the kingdom of Thronefall. They can be upgraded twice and upon construction or upgrade a research can be selected that buffs all units, all buildings, and the king.

The player can choose 1 out of 4 possible researches once the blacksmith is constructed or upgraded. Researches may be repeated multiple times.

Blacksmiths can be enhanced with the Faster Research perk.
