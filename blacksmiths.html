Blacksmiths
General

Blacksmiths are a protective building in the kingdom of Thronefall. They can be upgraded twice and upon or upgrade a research can be selected, that buffs all units, all buildings and the king.

The player can choose 1 out of 4 possible researches once the blacksmith is constructed or upgraded.

Blacksmiths can be enhanced with the Faster Research perk.
