Death God

General

The Challenge the God of Death mutator removes the ability for barracks and archery ranges to revive dead units and also increases the kings respawn time drastically, but also increases the score and XP by 20% if you win.


Description

Allied units are no longer revived at night and your own revive time is increased by +100%, but you get 20% more points and XP if you win.
