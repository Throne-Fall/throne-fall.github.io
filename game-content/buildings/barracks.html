Barracks

General

Barracks are a defensive building in the kingdom of Thronefall. They can be upgraded twice, and upon construction 4 units are spawned. Units respawn at the barracks slowly during the night and instantly at dawn.

The player can choose 1 out of 4 possible unit-types that are spawned by the barracks.

Barracacks can be enhanced with the Gladiator School perk.

Locations
