Royal Forge

General

Royal Forges are a protective building in the kingdom of Thronefall. They can be upgraded twice and upon construction or upgrade a research can be selected that buffs the king.

The player can choose 1 out of 4 possible researches once the Royal Forge is constructed or upgraded. Researches may be repeated multiple times.

Royal Forges can be enhanced with the Faster Research perk.
