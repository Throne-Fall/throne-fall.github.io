Tower

General

Towers are a defensive building in the kingdom of Thronefall. They can be upgraded twice and fire arrows at the closest enemy in range.

Towers can be enhanced with the Arcane Towers and Power Tower Perks.

The player can choose 1 out of 4 possible upgrades upon upgrading the Tower to level 3.
