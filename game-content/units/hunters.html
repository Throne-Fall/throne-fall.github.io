Hunters

General

Hunters are a ranged unit in Thronefall, specialising in fighting monster type enemies. They are unlocked upon reaching level 19 and can be recruited everywhere in the kingdom of Thronefall.

Hunters are the only ranged unit in the game that has a dedicated attack for melee range.

Description

Hunters are fast and effective against monsters. They have decent self defense capeabilities in melee combat, but are vulnerable against ranged units.
