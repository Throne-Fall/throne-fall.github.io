Lizzard Rider

General

Lizzard Riders are a hybrid melee and ranged hero in Thronefall, dealing huge single target damage.

Description

Fast archer unit that focusses on important targets like low health enemies, flying monsters or enemy support units. Additionally the lizzard can perform a melee attack.
