Sturmklamm

Overview

Sturmklamm (German for "Storm Revine") is the fifth map in the game.

The map features a mountain design, 2 new buildings, 3 new enemies and a new boss. It is also the first map with ambient effects (rain).


Map composition

This map features many slim corridors and paths up a hill, on which your castle center stands. In the south are 4 bridges, which represent the 4 spawnpoints of ground enemies. Since one bridge is not longer standing, its spawnpoint can only be used to spawn mole-like enemies. The north is blocked by hills, where multiple spawnpoints for flying enemies are located.
