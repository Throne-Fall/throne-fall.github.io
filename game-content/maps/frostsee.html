Frostsee

Overview

Frostsee (German for "Frost lake") is the fourth map in the game.

The map features a snowy tundra design, 2 new buildings, 4 new enemies and a boss. It is also the currently hardest map playable.

Map composition

This map features wide open areas, a few lakes and a buildable outpost. Everything is surrounded by icy mountains and water, resulting in 5 spawn areas, one being an ice cave.
