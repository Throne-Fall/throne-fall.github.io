War Horse

General

The War Horse Perk is unlocked upon reaching level 21 and increases the movementspeed of your king. It also eneables your king to deal damage by running over enemies.


Description

Your horse is +20% faster and riding over enemies deals 2.25 damage. This damage increases in correlation to all of your other in-game weapon upgrades like certain castle center upgrades.
