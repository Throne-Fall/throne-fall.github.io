Castle Fortifications

General

The Castle Fortifications Perk is unlocked upon reaching level 7 and increases the damage output of your caslte center.


Description

Your castle center has +150% health. The interval between its attacks is reduced by -67% and its attack range is increased by +50%.

Statistics

The castle gains +150% health points, +50% attack range and +200% attacks per second.
