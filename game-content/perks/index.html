Perks

Description

Perks are modifiers that can be selected before playing a level in Thronefall.

Perks give the player a certain boon during a level. At the start, only 1 perk can be selected. Up to 2 perks can be selected from player level 8 onwards, and 3 perks from player level 24 onwards.
