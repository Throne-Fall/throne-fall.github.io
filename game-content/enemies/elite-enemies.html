Elite Enemies

General

Elite Units are unique to the Eternal Trials and Elite God Mutator.

In the Eternal Trials they can be encountered as early as by stage 1, becoming more frequent on higher stages.

Details

While elite enemies do not change their behavior compared to their normal counterpart, they have increased health and damage. They are also highlighted by a yellow outline in-game and are announced before the night by a special icon. The icon is spiky on the outside and shows a golden version of their icon in the middle.
