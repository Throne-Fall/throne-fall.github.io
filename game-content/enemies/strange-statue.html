Strange Statue

General

Strange Statues are a unique boss-battle on the map Sturmklamm. On the final night of the map, the 4 statues on the map awaken and start to attack.


Description

Some speculate they could be part of an ancient defense mechanism? Maybe there is a reason this castle remained abandoned for so long? No... they are just statues...
