Hunterling

General

Hunterlings are a specialised enemy in Thronefall, as they target your king during the night. While not introduced in the tutorial, they can be encountered everywhere in the kingdom of Thronefall.

Description

Hunterlings are incredibly fast and dangerous monsters with the sole aim of chasing you down. They can smell your royal blood all across the entire kingdom.
