Slime

General

Slimes are a small enemy in Thronefall. They are encountered in the colder regions of the kingdom of Thronefall.

Description

Slimes are small melee monsters without much brain, so they will just attack whatever is closest to them. They usually appear in groups, which makes them vulnerable against splash damage.
