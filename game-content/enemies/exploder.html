Exploder

General

Exploders are a specialised enemy in Thronefall that die upon attacking a building, and explode upon death. They deal friendly fire to other enemies with their explosion. They are encountered in some outer regions of the kingdom of Thronefall.

Description

Exploders are monsters that roll straight for the closest building and explode when they die. They are slightly armored against attacks from the player character.
