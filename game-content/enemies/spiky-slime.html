Spiky Slime

General

Spiky Slimes are a small enemy in Thronefall. They are the bigger and healther version of the slime. They are encountered in the colder regions of the kingdom of Thronefall.

Description

Spiky Slimes are small melee monsters without much brain, so they will just attack whatever is closest to them. They usually appear in groups, which makes them vulnerable against splash damage. Spiky slimes have more health and attack than normal slimes.
