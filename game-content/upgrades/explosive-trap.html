Explosive Trap

General

Explosive Trap is an upgrade path of the Mill. It can be chosen upon construction of a mill and gives the mill the ability to deal damage to enemies.

Explosive Trap activates when the mill is destroyed and deals massive damage against all enemies nearby.

Description

The mill explodes when destroyed dealing massive damage around itself. The damage and radius increases every time you upgrade this mill.
