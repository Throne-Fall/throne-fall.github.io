Improved Plow

General

Improved Plow is an upgrade path of the Mill. It can be chosen upon construction of a mill and increases the amount gold the mill generates per night.

Improved Plow gives the mill +1 gold per night per upgrade.

Description

Every future upgrade of this mill will increase its income even more. (+1 / Upgrade)
