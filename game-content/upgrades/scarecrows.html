Scarecrows

General

Scarecrows is an upgrade path of the Mill. It can be chosen upon construction of a mill and gives the fields around the mill the ability to deal damage to flying enemies.

Scarecrows are build upon the construction of fields around the mill. These scarecrows deal massive damage against flying enemies nearby.

Description

Every field belonging to this mill receives a free scarecrow. Scarecrows have a strong anti-air attack.
