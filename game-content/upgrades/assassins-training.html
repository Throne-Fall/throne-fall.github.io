Assassins Training

General

Assassin's Training is an upgrade path of the Castle Center. It can be chosen upon upgrading the Castle Center to level 2 and increases the damage of the King significantly and may reduce the cooldown of your weapons active ability, when timed correctly.

Assassin's Training increases the Kings attack damage by +100% and can reduce your active abilities cooldown by up to 50%.
