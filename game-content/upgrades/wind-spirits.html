Wind Spirits

General

Wind Spirits is an upgrade path of the Mill. It can be chosen upon construction of a mill and gives the fields around the mill the ability to deflect arrows.

Description

Wind spirits magically intercept a portion of enemy arrows in the range of the mill. The higher the level of this mill, the more arrows are blocked.
