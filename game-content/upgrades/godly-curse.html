Godly Curse

General

Godly Curse is an upgrade path of the Castle Center. It can be chosen upon construction upgrading the Castle Center to level 3 and increases the damage an enemy takes, when it's targeted.

They player can target an enemy by pressing (left) alt.

Description

+If you lock in a target, that target receives 50% more damage from all sources +75% HP for your character.


The Godly Curse Icon.
