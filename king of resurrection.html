King of Resurrection
General

The King of Resurrection Perk is one of the currently 26 Perks in the game. It is unlocked upon reaching level 9 and grants your king one very fast revive each night.


Description

You are granted one very fast revive every night.

Statistics

The first revive of the King each night takes 2 seconds. This also works while using the Challenge the God of Death mutator.
