Neuland
Overview

Neuland (German for "New Land") is the Tutorial of Thronefall. It is one of the 4 maps available in the Early Access version.

It teaches the Player basic mechanics like attacking, building and upgrading.
Neuland is the easiest map of the game. As a tutorial, it has only one enemy spawning point located in the west.

Map composition

This map features a small plateau and a ramp leading to the enemy spawn point. Everything is surrounded by cliffs, a river and a forest.
