Neuland
Overview

Neuland (german for "New Land") is the Tutorial of Thronefall.
It is one of the 2 levels available in the demo version.

It teaches the Player basic mechanics like attacking, building, upgrading and more.
Neuland is the easiest of the game, having extra Regeneration as part of tutorial mechanics and only having one enemy spawning point on the west of the map.

Level composition

This level features a small plateau, a ramp leading to the enemy spawn point, surroundedd by cliffs, a river and a forest.
