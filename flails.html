Flails
General

Flails are a melee unit in Thronefall. They are unlocked upon reaching level 6 and can be recruited everywhere in the kingdom of Thronefall.

Flails are the only melee unit, that can damage multiple enemies at once.

Description

Flails can simultaniously attack all enemies in a circle around them.
